Jewels By the Sea

E-commerce website redesign | 8 weeks | 2023

Overview

Jewels By The Sea, a boutique in La Jolla, California that specializes in selling eclectic and unique jewelry, has not updated their website in 7 years. The owner is retiring and closing her physical location, but still wants to continue selling her jewelry pieces in nearby farmer's markets and online.


My Role: I was 1 of 4 contracted student designers and was responsible for communicating with our client, redesigning the website's home page, about page, navigation bar, and footer. I also, alongside my teammates, contributed equally in all other parts of design process.

Tools Used: Figma, Google Suite, Discord, and Lots of snacks!

Problem

Jewel's By the Sea's has an outdated website and wishes to continue selling jewelry after their physical store closes in June 2023.

Goal

➞ Modernizing online presence to compensate for physical store closure, while still upholding the shop's personality and meeting all the business owner's needs

Outcomes

✓ Finalized Figma prototype of redesign + Summer job to develop the site!
