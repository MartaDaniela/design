The Inspiration Behind My Logo


A representation of my culture + me.

When I was ten, I had an innate urge to learn pottery. That summer, I took classes and enjoyed the deftness and patience involved in throwing and painting my clay. Pottery became my creative outlet for the summer and later a window to my Mexican heritage.

As I was thinking of potential logo ideas, I knew I wanted my logo to be a representation of my culture and myself. I decided upon this minimalistic vase with my initials, MDA, inside; with pottery being a widely practiced artform in my culture and how my prior experience with pottery intertwines with my current career path.

Like pottery, UI/UX Design requires deftness and plenty of patience.
