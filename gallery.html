Designed on Figma 04.21.2023

"For you"

My first billboard design features a hidden candid photograph of myself at work, overlaid with the word "YOU" written multiple times in white, blue, and black in an abstract font. The word "YOU." in a bolder font is then superimposed on top of the other "YOU"s. The piece embodies my commitment to creating designs that reflect the unique needs of each person.

Designed on Figma 04.23.2023

"La Galleria"

This piece invites viewers into Daniela's portfolio gallery through vibrant colors, abstract shapes, combination of Spanish and English text, and images of personal significance. The inclusion of pottery imagery pays homage to her culture heritage, as well as her experience learning pottery at the age of 10. Meanwhile, the graffiti-esque font serves as a nod to the vibrant street art culture that surrounded Daniela during her high school years in East LA. The image of the mirror serves as a powerful symbol of reflection, underscoring Daniela's deep commitment to carefully considering every decision that goes into her designs.
